<h1>CoolChat</h1> 
A Chatting website through which you can chat with your friends or others. It Uses Socket IO and Express for handling connections and Backend respectively. The Frontend part is handled using Pug.

In the project directory, you can run:

### `npm start`

Runs the app in the development mode.\
Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) to view it in your browser.

The page firstly asks your name, after that you will join the chat with that name.\
Then you can chat with all others, who have joined the chat.\
You can also change the background of your chat, by the background changing button provided at the top right corner of your chat.
